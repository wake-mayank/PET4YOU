World's Oldest and Famous Dogs

Bobi - The Oldest Dog

Bobi (born 11 May 1992) is a male purebred Rafeiro do Alentejo dog from Portugal, confirmed to be 31 years old. His longevity is attributed to a loving home environment, a healthy diet, and regular veterinary care. Bobi's story has inspired many pet owners worldwide to cherish their furry companions.

Max - The Record Holder

Max, a Labrador retriever, holds the record for the longest jump by a dog at 30 feet 1 inch. His incredible leap showcases the strength and agility of the breed. Max has inspired many to engage in active play with their pets and train for new challenges.

Spike - The Senior Dachshund

Spike, a 23-year-old dachshund from Ohio, USA, is recognized as one of the oldest dachshunds in the world. His playful attitude and charming personality have made him a local favorite, and he enjoys sharing his adventures on social media, inspiring other pet owners.

Augie - The Oldest Golden Retriever

Augie, a Golden Retriever from Tennessee, USA, is currently 20 years old, setting a record for the breed's longevity. His loving and playful nature has made him a beloved family member, and he enjoys spending time with children and other pets.

Sako - The Wilderness Survivor

Sako, a King Shepherd, became famous for surviving in the Canadian wilderness for 40 days while protecting his owner. His bravery and loyalty in a challenging situation captivated the hearts of many and highlighted the incredible bond between dogs and humans.

Brandy - The Old Spaniel

Brandy, a cocker spaniel from the UK, lived for 28 years, making her one of the longest-lived spaniels in history. Known for her gentle demeanor, Brandy brought joy to her family and often participated in community events to promote dog adoption and welfare.

TobyKeith - The Chihuahua Senior

TobyKeith, a chihuahua from Florida, USA, celebrated his 21st birthday in 2023, one of the oldest dogs currently alive. He enjoys leisurely walks and spending time with his family. TobyKeith's lively spirit and playful nature have made him an adored companion for many.
